Weather Forecasts

Three day forecasts

AEMet is the Spanish national weather forecaster and the most reliable of sources. Forecasts are available for a number of locations around the island. Often you will have to check out more than one area to get an idea for the walk you propose.

Seven-day and hourly forecasts are also provided by AEMet.

Roque de Muchachos

Better forecasts for walks along the Caldera crest can be got from Mountain-forecast.com

Precipitation maps

Sometimes rain is forecast but very little falls and it's not worth cancelling a walk for. AEMet also provides a rainfall radar map which gives a better idea of what to expect than the summary forecasts.

Other forecasts

Dust

The Barcelona Dust Forecast Center has an animated dust map that may be useful for people with respiratory problems or when the Calima is blowing.

Fire risk, wind etc.

A series of maps (supposedly refreshed every 10 minutes) showing fire risk, temperature, humidity and other measures (in Spanish) is available from the council weather service.
